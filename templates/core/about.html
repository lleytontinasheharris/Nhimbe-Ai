About Nhimbe AI

Nhimbe is a Shona word for a traditional cooperative farming practice where community members come together to help each other in the fields.

Nhimbe AI carries this spirit into the digital age. We combine artificial intelligence with community knowledge to help smallholder farmers in Zimbabwe access the information they need to grow better crops and improve their livelihoods.

Our Mission

To bridge the agricultural information gap for Zimbabwean smallholder farmers by providing accessible, localized, and AI-powered farming advice.

What We Offer

  • AI-powered farming advice tailored to Zimbabwean conditions
  • Community forums for sharing farming knowledge
  • Curated knowledge base with local farming resources
  • Support for English, Shona, and Ndebele

Tirimubasa pamwe chete — We are working together.
